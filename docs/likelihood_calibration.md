# Observation-likelihood calibration

`fit_observation_likelihood.py` estimates multiplicative observation scales for the four surveillance channels by maximizing the negative-binomial likelihood on the first 70% of the integrated German panel. The biological transition model and dispersion parameters are held fixed; therefore these are observation-model calibration estimates, not a refit of the epidemic dynamics. The fitted scales are written to `data/processed/germany_likelihood_calibration.json` and should be applied only after checking the held-out predictive score. The upper-bound solutions for deaths, ICU, and hospitalization indicate that the current particle predictions are systematically low on the training split and that a joint state/parameter recalibration remains necessary before publication-level inference.
